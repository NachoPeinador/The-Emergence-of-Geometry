\begin{equation}
    \zeta(0) = -\frac{1}{2}
\end{equation}
In the context of MST, we interpret this value not as a mathematical curiosity, but as an \textbf{information potential}. The negative sign denotes an attractive nature (vacuum cohesion), and the $1/2$ factor indicates an intrinsic relationship with binary encoding (bits). It constitutes what we call the \textbf{half-bit attractor}, a state of pure information devoid of geometry.

\subsection{Analytical Derivation of the Master Identity}

Consider the complex logarithm of this ground state. In the complex plane, the logarithm of a negative number $z = -x$ (where $x > 0$) is multi-valued:
\begin{equation}
    \ln(-x) = \ln(x) + i(\pi + 2k\pi), \quad k \in \mathbb{Z}
\end{equation}

For the vacuum ground state, we postulate that observable physics corresponds to the \textbf{principal branch} of the logarithm ($k=0$), which minimizes phase action and ensures the uniqueness of the physical vacuum.

Applying this to $\zeta(0) = -1/2$:
\begin{align}
    \ln(\zeta(0)) &= \ln\left(-\frac{1}{2}\right) \nonumber \\
    &= \ln\left(\frac{1}{2}\right) + i\pi \nonumber \\
    &= (\ln 1 - \ln 2) + i\pi \nonumber \\
    &= -\ln 2 + i\pi
    \label{eq:log_zeta}
\end{align}
Here we observe the simultaneous emergence of two fundamental components:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Real Component ($-\ln 2$):} Represents pure information entropy (the negative Shannon bit, or negentropy).
    \item \textbf{Imaginary Component ($i\pi$):} Represents the emergent geometric phase.
\end{itemize}

Solving for $\pi$ in Equation \eqref{eq:log_zeta}, we obtain the structural identity linking geometry and information:
\begin{equation}
    i\pi = \ln(\zeta(0)) + \ln 2
\end{equation}
Multiplying both sides by $-i$ (recalling that $-i \cdot i = 1$):
\begin{equation}
    \boxed{\pi = -i \left[ \ln(\zeta(0)) + \ln 2 \right]}
    \label{eq:master_identity}
\end{equation}

\subsection{Ontological Interpretation: Geometry is Phase}

Equation \eqref{eq:master_identity} constitutes the central result of this work. Its ontological implication is profound: $\pi$ is not a fundamental ingredient of the universe. 

\begin{identidad}[Geometric Emergence]
